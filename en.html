About Me

My brief background: I graduated as a BA in Linguistics and Translation Studies at Moscow City University, then studied at the University of Bayreuth (dual degree program) and graduated as an MA in Linguistics with honors. After having returned to Moscow, I worked in various institutions until a task to construct and maintain a website on a simple webpage constructor (on the current job) clued me to extensively study web development and ultimately change the sphere of expertise.

Having successfully completed htmlacademy.ru intensive courses, a powerful educational platform, heightened my interest towards web technologies. I have eventually decided to change my area of expertise.

Professional growth is obviously of primary importance for me. My essential task is to grow from a rookie to a professional. My current goal is to join the IT crowd, work for the domestic IT industry, and continuie studies in frontend development.
